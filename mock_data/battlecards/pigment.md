# Battlecard — Pigment

> Last updated: 2026-04-22
> Owner: Competitive Intel Lead
> Status: ACTIVE — high frequency competitor in deal cycles

## Snapshot

- **HQ:** Paris, France
- **Founded:** 2019
- **Employees:** ~550
- **Last raise:** $145M Series D (Sept 2025, ICONIQ + IVP)
- **Total raised:** $393M
- **Where they win:** Series B+ tech, finance teams of 5–50, design-conscious buyers
- **Where we win:** Mid-market with Excel-heavy finance teams, faster time-to-value, transparent pricing

---

## Positioning (How They Pitch)

"Modern, AI-native business planning. The opposite of Anaplan: collaborative, fast, beautiful." They lean heavily on the AI assistant launched in Q4 2025 and on visual scenario modelling.

Their core narrative in 2026: *"Finance is being rebuilt for the AI era — and Pigment is the platform of that rebuild."*

---

## Strengths (Be Honest — Don't Underestimate)

1. **Scenario modelling UX is genuinely best-in-class.** Multi-version comparison, drag-to-adjust drivers, board-ready visuals out of the box.
2. **AI assistant (launched Q4 2025).** Natural-language scenario creation. Demos very well, even if production usage is still shallow.
3. **Brand momentum.** Backed by ICONIQ; high finance-Twitter share of voice; product-led marketing engine.
4. **Native multi-dimensional cube.** Outscales spreadsheet-style tools when models get complex (>10 dimensions).
5. **Design-first.** Wins evaluations where the buyer is a design-aware modern CFO.

---

## Weaknesses (Where We Win)

1. **Implementation 6–10 weeks.** Despite marketing, real go-lives still need a partner or a 1-FTE-equivalent finance hire. **We average 3–5 weeks for comparable scope.**
2. **Pricing opacity.** Quote-only, every deal bespoke. Buyers report frustration. **We publish starting tiers.**
3. **Limited integrations outside the core ERP set.** Strong NetSuite/SAP, weak on European-native ERPs (Sage, Odoo). **We integrate natively with both.**
4. **Steep learning curve for non-finance users.** Ops teams report "Pigment is for finance, not for the rest of the business."
5. **EU data residency.** Hosted on AWS, primary regions US-East and EU-West. For Spanish/German buyers wanting local residency, ask about their data plane explicitly.

---

## Typical Objections & Responses

**Objection: "Pigment has the AI assistant — you don't."**
Response: We do — but we don't build a feature against an AI demo unless our users ask for it. In customer interviews, scenario assistants have <8% weekly active usage even at Pigment. We invested instead in close acceleration, where weekly usage is 100%. Happy to walk through our roadmap.

**Objection: "Pigment is the modern choice — you're more traditional."**
Response: That's the marketing. The product reality: we ship every two weeks, our last release added [X] and [Y]. Let's compare changelogs side-by-side over the last 90 days.

**Objection: "Our investor uses Pigment, they recommended it."**
Response: Investor recommendations are valuable. Worth asking which finance team there is using it — usage often differs from procurement choice. We're happy to run a parallel pilot for 2 weeks, no contract.

## Win Stories (Anonymised)

### "Series C SaaS, 280 employees, Barcelona"
Evaluated Pigment + us in Q3 2025. Pigment quoted €92k ARR plus €40k implementation. We came in at €54k ARR, 4-week implementation, no partner needed. Champion was the new VP Finance who needed to show value to the CFO inside Q1. **Outcome: Won. ARR: €54k. Time to value: 26 days.**

### "Mid-market HR Tech, 350 employees, Stockholm"
Pigment had been pitching for 6 months. Buyer fatigue. We ran a 14-day pilot with their actual data — they saw a working close cycle on day 8. **Outcome: Won. ARR: €78k.**

### "Where Pigment Wins"
Series D+ companies, finance teams of 25+, multi-entity with strong central ops. We don't fight on this terrain unless we have a champion. Qualify out early.
